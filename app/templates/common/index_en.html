TERMS & CONDITIONS

Rules

Part 1

Code Puzzle

Security knowledge

Coding (PHP, Java, Ruby...): solve numerical mathematics questions using programming languages

Google and problem-solving skills

After answering each question successfully, your team will be given a special string, submit it to the system.

Part 2

Code Contest

Each team is required to write a program using a language of choice to solve the problem.

Send your code to the system, run through the test cases, only correct answers will be accepted and scored.

Part 3

AI Tournament

Create a Bot according to given rules with formatted input/output.

Periodically, tournaments between Bots are launched to compete 1:1 and allocate rankings.

Scores are calculated by respective rankings.

Evaluation & Grading

Once you solve the problems, send your answers to http://codewar.framgia.vn for grading.

The team which achieves higher total score after 3 parts is the winner. In condition of equal scores, the team who achieves the score earlier will be the winner.

Participants

Limited to 20 teams. First come first take !
